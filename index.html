ART CLUB

This is a online community art project.

Art Club was started as a way to encourage all ages and abilities to get sketching. Often due to family,work commitments, or just the fact that life gets in the way we don't make time to be creative or can't go to a class easily. Our online community makes it easy to share your work anytime and keep motivated with regular sketch challenges and encouragment from other members.

Join our facebook community for regular sketch challenges and to share your work and see the work of our other members. Follow and share on Twitter and instagram. Sign up to our monthly newsletter for useful tips, community news, and events.

"Every child is born an artist, the challenge is to remain one when we grow up" Pablo Picasso
